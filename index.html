Privacy Policy

Tracking Life - Workout Tracker
Last updated: February 14, 2026

Tracking Life does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your workout data stays on your device.

Data Storage

  • All workout logs, exercise programs, and preferences are stored locally on your device only.
  • No user accounts or sign-ups are required to use the app.
  • No data is sent to any external server or cloud service.
  • Your fitness data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Tracking Life uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing: Used to process in-app purchases. Google handles all payment information according to their own privacy policy. We do not receive, access, or store your payment details.

Data Collection

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Any health or fitness data beyond what you enter into the app (which stays on your device)

Data Deletion

  • Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all stored data from your device.
  • You can also clear the app's data at any time through your device's Settings > Apps > Tracking Life > Clear Data.

Children's Privacy

Tracking Life does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for users of all ages as it does not collect any personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
